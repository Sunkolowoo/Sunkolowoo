Services We Offer At Friendly Nurses Home Health Care, we offer a wide range of services designed to support the diverse needs of our clients. Our experienced team of skilled professionals is committed to providing personalized care that fosters health, independence, and overall well-being.Explore our comprehensive services below

Skilled Nursing

Our Registered Nurses (RNs) and Licensed Vocational Nurses (LVNs) provide expert care for variousmedical needs, including wound care, medication management, pre-operative and post-operative care, diabetic teaching, and more. Our nursing staff works closely with clients, families, and physicians to develop a customized care plan that promotes optimal health and recovery.

Home Health Aide

Our compassionate Certified Home Health Aides (CHHAs) provide personal care and assistance with daily activities, such as bathing, grooming, and meal preparation. Under the supervision of our skilled nursing staff, home health aides ensure that clients can live comfortably and safely in their own homes.

Physical Therapy

Our Physical Therapists (PTs) assess and treat functional impairments resulting from injury, stroke, surgery , or other medical conditions. Through personalized exercise programs, mobility training, and pain management techniques, our therapists help clients regain strength, mobility, and independence.

Speech Therapy

Our Speech Therapists, or Speech-Language Pathologists (SLPs), create treatment plans to improve speech and communication challenges, as well as evaluate and treat swallowing disorders. Through targeted therapy techniques, our speech therapists support clients in achieving clearer communication and enhanced quality of life.

Occupational Therapy

Our Occupational Therapists (OTs) evaluate and develop rehabilitative care plans for patients needing assistance with activities of dai ly living and independence. By addressing physical, cognitive, and environmental factors, our therapists help clients regain the skills necessary to perform daily tasks and maintain a fulfilling life.

Social Services

Our Medical Social Workers (MSWs) and care counselors act as facilitators, connecting clients with necessary community and supportive services, such as transportation and meal programs. In addition to providing emotional support and improving coping skills, our social service professionals help clients navigate complex healthcare and social systems, ensuring they receive the resources they need.

    Post-Surgical Care: Assisting with wound care, medication administration, and physical therapy to ensure a smooth recovery process.
    Chronic Disease Management: Monitoring and managing conditions like diabetes, hypertension, and COPD, including medication management and lifestyle advice.
    Palliative and Hospice Care: Providing compassionate support for patients with serious illnesses, focusing on pain relief, comfort, and emotional support for both patients and families.
    Daily Living Assistance: Helping with personal care activities such as bathing, dressing, and mobility to maintain independence and quality of life.
    Medication Management: Ensuring accurate medication administration, monitoring for side effects, and coordinating with physicians to adjust treatme nts as needed.

Our goal is to deliver tailored, high-quality care that meets the specific needs of each patient, enhancing their overall well-being and quality of life.
